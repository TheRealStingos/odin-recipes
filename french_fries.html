French Fries Recipe

This is a recipe for French Fries

Ingredients

  • One third cup white sugar
  • two cups warm water
  • two large russet potatoes - peeled and sliced into strips
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium bown, dissolve the sugar in warm water. Soak potatoes in water mixture for 15 minutes. Remove from water, and dry thoroughly on paper towels
  2. Hear oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Add potatoes, and cook until golden, 5 to 6 minutes. Drain on papter towels. Season with salt to taste
